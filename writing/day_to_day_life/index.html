Nostalgia

When I am bored, I usually look through old photos in our family photo album. What I am most surprised about however isn't what I was doing that day, whether it be a new Lego build or something else equally frivolous, but what was in the background of the photo. These small details, such as where we stored cereal boxes or our old dining table really do give you a sense of just how much you have forgotten about in the past.
