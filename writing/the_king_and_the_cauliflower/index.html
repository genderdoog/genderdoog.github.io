The King and the Cauliflower

Written by: Matthew

Originally posted: 18th of November, 2025
Last updated: 18th of November, 2025

Short story

There once was a king who could turn any inanimate object sentient.

One day, the king made a piece of cauliflower on his dinner plate come to life.

The cauliflower told the king that it was jealous of his rival, broccoli.

"Nobody picks cauliflower over broccoli", the little one exclaimed, "This isn't fair!

The king laughed.

"You silly one, peasants choose to grow and eat you. Even I, as king of this land, am eating cauliflower right now."

"You all didn't choose, though. A thief stole all the broccoli seeds last year. Your castle chefs decide what you eat."

"Don't be irrational," stated the king, and swallowed the cauliflower whole.
