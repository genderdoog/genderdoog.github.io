Team Fortress 2

Written by: Matthew

Originally posted: 19th of April, 2024
Last updated: 30th of June, 2025

Computer games

Team Fortress 2 is my favourite video game. With over 600 hours in this game spanning a third of my life, it has consume me in a way in which I could never imagine.

I don't remember when I started playing. Back then it was different period for me, one in which I remember watching Minecraft videos unironically.

Now that I think about it now, I shouldn't have even played it in the first place when I was that young. But that doesn't really matter now, does it?

What I remember from those early years is still stuck in my mind all these years later. 23 bots on pl_upward, playing as the RED engineer.

I wasn't very good at aiming shotguns and rockets, so my engineer turtling lifestyle was all I had to get those sweet sweet bot kills.

It was especially intense when the payload was moving just into line of sight of my sentry, where all 8 bots were dancing around the cart.

From an outsiders perspective, this sounds horribly boring. But for me, it was exhilarating experience. From the first mouse and keyboard inputs to the last, I had a lot of fun.

I played games like this for hours on end, weekend after weekend.

Today, I play on Skial ctf_2fort, with the occasional casual matchmaking server. Its a shame that the bot crisis has gotten to this point, where community servers are our only hope in playing interruption free.

With no major updates within 7 year, its a miracle that Team Fortress 2 has an active player base to this day, 17 years after its release on the Orange Box.
