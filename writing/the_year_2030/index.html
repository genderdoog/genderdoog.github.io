The year 2030

Written by: Matthew

Originally posted: 24th of February, 2024
Last updated: 30th of June, 2025

Fiction writing

It is 02:00.

The moon casts a harsh white light on the bench, situated behind a lush forest.

Smells of burnt plastic and waterlogged wood permiate throughout the air.

A murder of crows screech nearby.

Ambulance sirens in the distance drown out white noise.
