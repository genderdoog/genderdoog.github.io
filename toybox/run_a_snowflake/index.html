Run a Snowflake in your browser

To get started, press the "enabled" toggle below.

By enabling your Snowflake, you are contributing to the Tor network, allowing users in heavily censored countries to access the open web (assuming you are not already in such a country).

Feel free to leave your Snowflake running in the background while you work on other stuff, just be sure not to close this tab!
